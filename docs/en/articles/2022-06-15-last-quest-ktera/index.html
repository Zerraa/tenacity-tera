30/6/2022 - Official TERA Shutdown

Unfortunately, TERA’s developers, Bluehole, have decided to cease work on TERA. The TERA servers will be switched off at 10 AM CEST (4 AM EDT) on 30th June 2022. After nearly 10 long years, the TERA legend must sadly come to an end. Please note that the game cannot be added in the Gameforge client anymore, and in case you have it installed, its recommended for you to uninstall it after the server closing.

20/6/2022 - Menma’s TERA goes 64-bit

The transition to 64-bit is almost complete. The 64-bit testing environment will be going public for a few days to check for potential issues and bugs with it. Everyone will be able to access the 64-bit test server by downloading the 64-bit launcher and game client from the link in downloads section on Menma’s Discord You can use your existing account to connect to the server and you will be having your current progress from the main server.

15/6/2022 Last Quest

If you access the Korean servers by 30th June, you can play Last Quest, created by the BHS as a final Goodbye before the live servers officially shut down. It was confirmed that Gameforge (EU,NA,SEA publisher) will NOT be releasing this questline themself. As of writing, they only released translated cutscene from last part of the series of quests. But honestly, their translated version is as cheap as it can get…
